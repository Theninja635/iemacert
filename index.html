Is IEMA Accredited?

IEMA is fully accredited and recognised for management systems and products conformity assessments all over the world.

How long is the first certificate renewable?

The first certificate issued to your organisation is for 3 years, with annual surveillance audits to ensure continued compliance.

How is Preassessment audit conducted?

Our assessors determine your readiness by assessing systems, operations and documentation, then issue a report advising the next step.
