About IEMA

Company overview

IEMA Standards Limited is an Egyptian Accreditation Council (EGAC) accredited Certification Body, based in Nigeria, the United States and the United Kingdom. Established in 2015, we are extensively engaged in ISO standards certification, inspection and testing for management systems, products and services all over the world.

We have over 100 experts with experience in various industries that conduct specialised assessments, audits and certifications in accordance with ISO/IEC 17021-1 and IAF compliance.

At IEMA our teams will help your organisation with all required ISO certification needs, including product and equipment testing, inspection and certification all over the world.
